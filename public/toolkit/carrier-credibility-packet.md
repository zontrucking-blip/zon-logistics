## Why Work Directly With Us

Every time you book freight through a broker, a portion of what you pay goes to someone who never touched your load. The industry average runs between 15 and 25 percent. On a $3,000 load, that's somewhere between $450 and $750 that moved no freight.

[Company Name] is the carrier of record on every load we move. Our drivers work for us. Our trucks are ours. When you call to check on your freight, you're talking to the person who dispatched it, not someone waiting on a callback from the actual carrier.

If something goes wrong on a load, one phone call handles it. No broker in the middle, no diffusion of responsibility, no "I'll check with the carrier and get back to you."

**What you get working with us directly:**

- The rate goes to the truck, not a middleman
- One contact for pickup, transit, and delivery
- Direct line to dispatch and the driver
- Faster claims resolution if something goes wrong
- A carrier that picks up the phone

We're not the right fit for every shipper. If you need 40 trucks a week across 12 states, we're probably not your answer. But if you've got consistent lanes, you value communication, and you're tired of paying extra for a broker who routes your calls, we're worth a conversation.
